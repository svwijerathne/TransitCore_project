Process

Four steps to seamless commuting

Registration establishes your profile as driver or passenger. The system encrypts sensitive data and applies role-based access controls from the start.

1. Register

Create your account and choose your role. Identity verification happens immediately through institutional email validation.

2. Search

Browse available routes or publish your own. The matching engine filters candidates by safety ratings and trust criteria.

3. Ride

Complete your trip with real-time delay notifications and multi-modal journey tracking. The system monitors every leg.

4. Settle

Fares calculate and debit instantly through atomic transactions. Drivers receive points while passengers see structured cost breakdowns.

Payouts

Liquidate earnings through multiple channels

Drivers accumulate points with every completed trip. TransitCore Ledger System lets you convert those earnings into cash or merchant credit through secure, interchangeable payout strategies.

Bank transfer routing

Offload your balance directly to external accounts using verified routing numbers. Transactions execute through synchronized ledger calls with zero direct wallet manipulation.

ATM voucher generation

Convert points into encrypted PIN codes for terminal cash dispensing. Short-lived vouchers ensure security while maintaining operational simplicity.

Merchant store checkout

Spend points directly at partner vendors through secure transaction handshakes. Your wallet integrates smoothly with third-party e-commerce platforms.
