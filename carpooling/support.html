Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How can I find a ride?

Go to the Find Ride option and search available rides based on your route.

❓ How can I become a driver?

Register your account and select Driver as your user role.

❓ How can I cancel my ride?

You can cancel your booking from your trip management section.
